A bootcamp is an intensive, short-term training program designed to help participants acquire specific skills quickly, often in high-demand fields like coding, data science,or digital marketing. These programs are tailored for individuals looking to switch careers, enhance their expertise, or gain hands-on experience in a new field. Bootcamps typically combine practical projects, mentorship, and real-world applications to provide a focused learning environment. emphasize practical skills over theoretical knowledge, making them ideal for people who want to enter the job market or advance in their careers swiftly. Whether online or in-person, bootcamps are highly immersive and demand commitment, offering a fast-paced but rewarding pathway to professional growth.
